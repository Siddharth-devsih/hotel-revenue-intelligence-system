# Business Insight

## Executive Summary

Hotel booking revenue was converted from INR to USD using the Frankfurter Exchange Rate API to support international business reporting.

The analysis demonstrates how external financial data can be integrated with operational booking data to create more meaningful business intelligence for global stakeholders.

---

## Key Findings

- Revenue trends remain consistent after currency conversion.
- Automated exchange-rate integration eliminates manual conversion effort.
- USD reporting improves accessibility for international investors and management teams.
- External APIs can be effectively integrated into business analytics workflows.

---

## Business Interpretation

The ability to report revenue in multiple currencies improves financial transparency and enables better comparison across international markets.

Organizations operating globally require standardized reporting formats, and automated currency conversion helps ensure consistency and accuracy.

---

## Recommendations

1. Automate exchange-rate updates on a scheduled basis.
2. Expand reporting to support additional currencies such as EUR and GBP.
3. Integrate the reporting pipeline into business dashboards.
4. Implement historical exchange-rate tracking for trend analysis.

---

## Conclusion

The project demonstrates the practical value of combining external API data with internal business data. Automated multi-currency reporting improves reporting efficiency, supports global decision-making, and enhances financial visibility across markets.
